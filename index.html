About GeezX

Our Mission

To bridgr the digital divide by providing accessible, high quality tech education to youth from all backgrounds, empowering them to become creators of technology rather than just consumers.

Our Vision

A world where youth are equipped with the digital skills and mindset to solve complex problems, drive innovation, and create positive change in their communities through technology.

What We Do

We provide hands-on programming training, tech workshops, hackathons, mentorship programs, and career development opportunities for young people interested in technology.
